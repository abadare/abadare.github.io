About Me

Hey! I'm Alex Tong Lin, and I am currently an Assistant Adjunct Professor at UCLA Mathematics. My research interests are in machine learning, deep learning, multi-agent reinforcement learning, and mean-field games.

Quick bio: I was born and raised in San Francisco, and am currently under the mentorship of Professor Stanley J. Osher.
